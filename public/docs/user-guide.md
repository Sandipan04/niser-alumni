# 📖 User Guide

Welcome to the NISER Alumni Archive! This platform is designed to connect students, researchers, and faculty while making it easy to find peers across different graduating batches.

## 🔎 Searching & Filtering
The public directory is highly optimized to help you find the exact person you're looking for.
1.  **Sidebar Navigation:** Click a batch or department in the left sidebar to instantly scroll to that group.
2.  **Fuzzy Search:** Type parts of a name, research interest, or institute in the top search bar. The grid will filter instantly.
3.  **Dropdown Filters:** Combine dropdowns to find specific demographics (e.g., `Int. MSc.` + `Start Year: 2021`).

## ✏️ Managing Your Profile
To maintain data integrity, public users cannot directly write to the live database. All changes are routed through a verification queue.

### 1. Joining the Network
Click **Join Network** in the sidebar. Fill out the application form. You can crop and upload a profile photo directly in the browser. Once submitted, your profile enters the pending queue. Once an Admin approves it, you will go live.

### 2. Updating Your Profile
If your profile exists but needs updates (e.g., new job, new email):
* Click **DM Admin** in the sidebar.
* Provide your name, batch, and exactly what information needs to be updated. The admins will manually update your record from the secure dashboard.

### 3. Direct Messaging (DMs)
Use the DM Admin button for data removal requests, corrections, or platform feedback. Messages are sent directly to the administrators' dashboard and Discord channel.
