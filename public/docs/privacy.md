# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 2026

Your privacy and data security are important. This policy explains what data we collect, how it is stored, and how it is used within the NISER Alumni Archive.

## 1. Information We Collect
When you submit a profile or an update request, we collect the following information:
* **Required Data:** Full name, programme, department, start and end years, and a permanent email address.
* **Optional Data:** Current professional position, institute, research interests, social media links, phone numbers, secondary emails, and a profile photo.
* **Communication Data:** If you use the DM feature, we collect the content of your message, your name, and your batch.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
* **Public Display:** The core purpose of this platform is networking. By submitting your profile, you explicitly consent to having your provided academic, professional, and contact details displayed publicly on the internet.
* **Administrative Notifications:** Submitted forms (Joins, Updates, and DMs) trigger automated alerts to the administrative team to facilitate quick reviews.

## 3. Data Storage and Security
* **Infrastructure:** Your text data is securely stored in a Cloudflare D1 database, and profile photos are stored in Cloudflare R2 object storage.
* **Security:** We utilize industry-standard practices, including HTTPS encryption and sanitized data queries, to protect your information from unauthorized access.

## 4. Third-Party Sharing
We **do not** sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties. 
* System notifications (such as alert messages for new user joins or DMs) are routed through secure Discord webhooks strictly for administrative monitoring.

## 5. Your Rights Over Your Data
You retain full control over your data. You have the right to:
* **Update your data:** Request modifications to your profile using the "Update" button on your profile page.
* **Delete your data:** Request complete removal of your profile and uploaded photos from our active database and storage buckets by sending a DM to the admin.

## 6. Contact
If you have concerns about your data privacy or wish to exercise your data rights, please use the DM feature on the website to contact the platform administrator.
